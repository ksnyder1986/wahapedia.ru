A unit of Bestigors has any number of models, each armed with a Despoiler Axe.

Bestial Charge: Always at the forefront of a Brayherd, Bestigors launch themselves at their foes with unbridled ferocity.
Add 1 to the Attacks characteristic of this unit’s melee weapons in a turn in which it made a charge move.

You must make a battleshock roll for each friendly unit that has to take a battleshock test. If the battleshock roll is greater than the unit’s Bravery characteristic, the battleshock test has been failed. If the test is failed, for each point by which the battleshock roll exceeds the unit’s Bravery characteristic, 1 model in that unit must flee. You decide which models flee. A model that flees is removed from play.

If a slain model is returned to its unit in the same turn that it is slain, it still counts as having been slain in that battle round for the purposes of battleshock tests.
